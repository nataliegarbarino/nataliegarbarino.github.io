Concept Z

Beginnings at UC Berkeley

The first iteration of the Cheetah, Concept Z, was build on the roof of Etcheverry Hall on the UC Berkeley campus by engineering students James Osborne, Jon Garbarino, and Kevin Frantz.

What is Concept Z?

Concept Z, the human powered vehicle built at the University of California at Berkeley, is composed of a carbon-fiber/metal matrix bicycle frame, and aerodynamic Kevlar fairing.

In 1986 James Osborn, Jon Garbarino and Kevin Frantz joined the UC Berkeley Human Powered Vehicle Team, which was a group of 10-15 undergraduate engineers. Together they designed and built the Concept Z, Berkeley’s first semi-recumbent 2 wheeled HPV on the roof of Etcheverry Hall

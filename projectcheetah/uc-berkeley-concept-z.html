UC Berkeley Concept Z

The first iteration of the Cheetah, Concept Z, was built on the roof of Etcheverry Hall on the UC Berkeley campus by engineering students and members of the UCB Human Powered Vehicle team James Osborne, Jon Garbarino, and Kevin Frantz.
